Challenges

As a student in the university, my personal experience of the hassle of searching for credible tutorials based on lecture sessions is my core motivation for this project. The process of looking for a particular tutorial of interest based on individual institutions’ lecture sessions that may depict examples and explanations exclusively tackled during lecture is stressful and time-consuming without the promise of finding any. Eventually, even when a tutorial is being organized, it may be scheduled at an unsuitable time for a student. However, this problem can be solved using the available technology to provide these tutorials at the click of a button and make it downloadable to ones’ local storage devices in an even durable form that can withstand the test of time.
