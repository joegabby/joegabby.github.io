Challenges

As a student in the university, my personal experience of the hassle of searching for credible tutorials based on lecture sessions is my core motivation for this project. The process of looking for a particular tutorial of interest based on individual institutions’ lecture sessions that may depict examples and explanations exclusively tackled during lecture is stressful and time-consuming without the promise of finding any. Eventually, even when a tutorial is being organized, it may be scheduled at an unsuitable time for a student. However, this problem can be solved using the available technology to provide these tutorials at the click of a button and make it downloadable to ones’ local storage devices in an even durable form that can withstand the test of time.

Role

For this project, I played the role of Product Designer and Developer

Research Process

I took my first step into the reseach process by interviewing students around the campus about how they feel about tutorials.

I was mostly interested in the following areas:

  • How often students welcome the idea of tutorials (especially from peers)
  • How often students consume online tutorials and how enjoyable is it for them
  • Do students prefer physical tutorials to online tutorials?

Discovery

After carrying out my research on the importance and need of self administered tutorials i arrived at the following conclusions

  • Students sometimes prefer to be peer tutored
  • Some students considered watching tutorials as stressfull after a hard day of long lectures
  • Some students found it difficult to download tutorials directly to their local storage devices to avoid continous streaming and saving cost
  • Many students feel distant relationship between them and online tutors in common platforms as they are hardly able to relate with the tutors
  • Students who tutored also learn more as they teach

Deeper insight

having itemized my user needs, it was now time for me to match and cross reference them with my existing ideas. i had to trim down my first ideas of the system to really fit the target users needs; for one i actually intended to include a live streaming function on the app but at this stage i finalized it wasnt a feasible design on the app as many students wont always be chanced to hold a live tutorial session (both tutors and tutee alike)

Designing

having carried out my research and arrived at my end users needs, the design goals of the system became clearer. i now know i need a system that can help students upload tutorials at will in more than just one media and also enable the beneficiaries of this tutorial to be able to download these tutorials and hold a conversation with their tutors






i started out with the UI design using Adobe XD and i tried to fit in the many functionalities of the app neatly into the screen in a user friendly manner, i iterated between a lot of design options before finally finding the perfect fit for the project. i tried to make sure the user has access to all the features especially the most crucial one in less clicks i tried placing all the crucial buttons within the users reach
